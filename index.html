About GenderSafeUK

GenderSafeUK (gendersafeuk.co.uk) aims to help people find gender neutral and unisex bathrooms across the United Kingdom.

Our data is crowdsourced from users like you, as well as public information about gender neutral facilities.

If you know of a gender neutral bathroom that isn't on our map, please add it to help others!

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This website uses local storage on your device to remember your preferences and contributions.

We do not track your personal information or share any data with third parties.

Location data is only used to center the map and is not stored.
